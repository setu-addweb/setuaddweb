Welcome to Alpha College



The soul of Khatraj, Alpha College of Engineering & Technology, is established with the principle of nurturing students in the field of technology and innovation in the ambiance of liberty and harmony. Incepted from the roots of Alpha Education Foundation Trust, ACET has been moving at the forefront of advancement for nearly a half a decade, in the area of pivotal technologies and teaching methodologies. Started with 240 seats with the Bachelor Degree in Engineering Programme, the institute now has total intake of 924 in Electronics & Communication Engineering, Computer Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Information Technology.
