Welcome to ALPHACET:

"Liberty without Learning is always in peril and Learning without Liberty is always in vain."

The soul of Khatraj, Alpha College of Engineering & Technology, is established with the principle of nurturing students in the field of technology and innovation in the ambiance of liberty and
harmony. Incepted from the roots of Alpha Education Foundation Trust, ACET has been moving at the forefront of advancement for nearly a half a decade, in the area of pivotal technologies and
teaching methodologies. Started with 240 seats with the Bachelor Degree in Engineering Programme, the institute now has total intake of 924 in Electronics & Communication Engineering, Com-
puter Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Information Technology and Civil Engineering. The institute also offers a Postgraduate programme in Computer Engineering.
